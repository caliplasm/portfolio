Sound Healing

Healing through sound uses the tones and frequencies of tuning forks, drumming, crystal bowls Tibetan bowls, chimes, shakers and the voice to align the body and body parts to its original, harmonious blueprint.

Sound healing is used for both personal development and relaxation. Using biofield tuning techniques as well as sound healing techniques to remove any blocks of frequency and vibration within the body, sound healing may reduce stress, anxiety, improve sleep and create a deeper sense of wellbeing.

A session is 60 minutes. We set an intention of what the client would like to accomplish. Client is fully clothed and may sit in a chair or lay on a massage table. Session is available in person, on video or at a distance.
