Meet Cate


Born Intuitive, Author, Speaker, Curandera, Rapid Eye Technician, Brain Gym practitioner, Sound Healer and Yoga Therapist, Cate empowers her clients to appreciate and create the life they desire.

In childhood, Cate was introduced to alternative medicine and healing as her father underwent three surgeries, which caused him worse pain than his initial car accident. Her mother, trained as an Registered Nurse, learned and taught her about acupuncture, kinesiology, muscle testing, visual training, homeopathy, acupressure, Shamanic healing and so many other modalities.

At 18, Cate began to study and certify in many modalities. Having trained in Brain gym, Reiki, EFT-Tapping, Crucial Conversations, Crucial Confrontations, Journey work, Neurolinguistic Programming, hypnotherapy, Emotion Code, Body Code, Chakras, Morter March, Divine Intervention, Angel work, Meditation, Shamanism, Drumming, Advanced Sound healing, Biofield Tuning, Yoga therapy, Marconics, Resonance repatterning, Reflexology, Advanced Cranial Sacral, Rapid Eye Technology, Foot zoning, Access Consciousness and many others, she incorporates these tools in her work with her clients.

Having experienced over 600 modalities, Cate chose to train extensively in the ones she found to be the most effective and the best of the best. She continues to train, learn and incorporate new learnings with her clients.

Cate enjoys working with clients who desire change and are willing to move forward to create the life they desire. She assists her clients release stress and trauma from the brain and body so they can create the lives they want to live.
