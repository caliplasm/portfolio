Meet Cate

Born Intuitive, Author, Speaker, Curandera, Rapid Eye Technician, Brain Gym practitioner, Sound Healer and Yoga Therapist, Cate is on a mission to empower the planet to appreciate and create the life they desire.

In childhood, I was introduced to alternative medicine and healing as my father underwent three surgeries, which caused him worse pain than his initial car accident. My mother, trained as an RN, learned and taught me about acupuncture, kinesiology, muscle testing, visual training, homeopathy, acupressure, Shamanic healing and so many other modalities.

At 18, I began to study and certify in many modalities. Having trained in Brain gym, Reiki, EFT-Tapping, Crucial Conversations, Crucial Confrontations, Journey work, Neurolinguistic Programming, hypnotherapy, Emotion Code, Body Code, Chakras, Morter March, Divine Intervention, Angel work, Meditation, Shamanism, Drumming, Advanced Sound healing, Biofield Tuning, Yoga therapy, Marconics, Resonance repatterning, Reflexology, Advanced Cranial Sacral, Rapid Eye Technology, Foot zoning, Access Consciousness and many others, I incorporate these tools in my work with my clients.

Having experienced over 600 modalities, I have chosen to train extensively in the ones I’ve found to be the most effective and the best of the best. I continue to train and learn and incorporate new learnings with my clients.

I enjoy working with clients who desire change and are willing to move forward to create the life they desire. I assist my clients release stress and trauma from the brain and body so they can create the lives they want to live.

If you desire better--if you believe you can and are willing to do the work--you can.

I’d love to work with you and facilitate you in your healing journey. I love watching my clients transform and receive all they desire.
